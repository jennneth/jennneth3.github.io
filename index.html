Jen Johansson


Jen Johansson is an up and coming data scientist focused on making the world a better place for the next generation. Jen has worked in the localization industry for the last 15 years, but sees the industry changing as a result of advances in Machine Learning and AI. As a result, she is studying to become a data scientist by building a toolbox containing SQL, Python, and other languages.

Jen is a mother of three, and she sees the future as being very bright based on what she sees of this generation.
